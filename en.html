I teach English

"It is important for me to create an atmosphere where fears and doubts give way to inspiration and motivation to reach your goals."

I dreamed of becoming an English teacher (and for some reason English) since my 3rd grade at school. I remember coming home from school with my mom looking at a university across the street and saying, "One day I will become an English teacher and will work at university too."


That's exactly what happened a few years later: I entered Moscow State Linguistic University and immediately began working as a tutor. After graduation I worked at several universities. Now, however, I am working at a school, with talented and motivated teens. Teaching and languages are still a big and important part of my life.


Main areas of expertise:

  • General English
  • Academic English
  • IELTS (General, Academic Modules)
  • Cambridge exams (KET, FCE, CAE)
  • Russian State Exams

My journey from teaching English to psychology

"In the beginning psychology was a hope for a better life for me."

I have always been interested in psychology. Psychology was a hope for a better life for me: I read books, attended seminars, watched YouTube, had sessions with various psychologists a couple of times (honestly, not all of these things were related to real psychology) and kept looking for the magical key to solving all my problems!


During this journey I found my gestalt-psychotherapist. At that time, I didn't know what the gestalt approach was all about or how it worked. I simply trusted the specialist and began the work. I was working and the approach was working!


Most of my life I worked at universities, and in 2019 I started working at a school as a teacher and a tutor. The role of a tutor was absolutely new to me, so I started looking for some professional development in this sphere.


This is how I started my studies at Moscow Institute for Gestalt Therapy and Psychodrama. Now I am already in my third year (the second stage) and I have even started my practical part for future certification.


I am happy to share my journey as a teacher, mentor and future gestalt therapist with you on this page and in real life!
